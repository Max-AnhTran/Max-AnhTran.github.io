Top 5 places to visit in Helsinki

  1. Sea Fortress: A UNESCO World Heritage site, Suomenlinna is not only a historic fortress but also a beautiful archipelago with museums, parks, and panoramic views of the sea.
  2. Helsinki Cathedral: Dominating the skyline, this iconic cathedral in Senate Square is a masterpiece of neoclassical architecture and a symbol of Helsinki.
  3. Temppeliaukio Church (Rock Church): Carved into solid rock, this unique church is not only an architectural marvel but also known for its excellent acoustics and serene ambiance.
  4. Market Square (Kauppatori): Located by the harbor, this lively open-air market is a great place to experience local life, enjoy fresh Finnish produce, and savor delicious street food.
  5. Kiasma - Museum of Contemporary Art: For art enthusiasts, Kiasma offers a dynamic collection of contemporary Finnish and international art, providing insights into the modern art scene.

Transport

In Helsinki, there is a friendly-user public transport called HSL. It has trains, trams, metros, buses, and ferries. You only need to download the HSL app and then fill in the starting point and destination to find out routes.

FINLAND

Discover the enchanting beauty of Finland, a land of pristine landscapes and vibrant cities. From the magical Northern Lights in Lapland to the modern architecture of Helsinki, Finland offers a diverse range of experiences. Immerse yourself in the unique culture, explore picturesque lakes, and unwind in traditional saunas. With its friendly locals and rich history, Finland is a destination that promises both tranquility and excitement. Whether you're seeking outdoor adventures or cultural delights, Finland's charm captivates every traveler's heart.

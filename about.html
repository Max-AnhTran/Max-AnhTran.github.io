About us

Love traveling, I'm by your side

Wander often, wonder always

I am Max. Since I have a passion for traveling and have visited over 10 countries, I've come to understand the significance of meticulous planning and acquiring essential information about destinations before embarking on a journey.

Therefore, I created this website to help you guys solve this issue.

My website provides the most important information about destinations and some tips and notes that I have learned from my traveling experience.

I hope that "Travelling with Me" will become an indispensable companion for you throughout your journeys.

This is my website project in the course Digi-Intro
